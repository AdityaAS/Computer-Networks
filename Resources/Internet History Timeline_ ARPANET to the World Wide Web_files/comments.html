This information on "the radiated library" is based on Otlet's Traite de Documentation of 1934 with images from the EUM in the Archives of the Mundaneum in Mons. However, the concept of "the radiated library" was actually already announced in various lectures on the Future of the Book in the first two decades of the 20th Century (at least for radio). In 1909: Otlet spoke of "a universal network that would permit the dissemination of knowledge without limitation": in his contribution La Fonction et les transformations du Livre. Resume de la conference faite a la Maison du Livre. 14 Novembre 1908, Musee du Livre Publication no. 11: Bruxelles Musee du Livre, 1909, 33pp., page 29.
